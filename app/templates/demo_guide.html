Interview walk-through

A short script you can follow to impress the panel in under three minutes.

1. Launch the service

  1. Activate the virtual environment and run uvicorn app.main:app --reload.
  2. Explain that FastAPI exposes both the REST API and the light management UI from one codebase.
  3. Mention that the QA + NER models load once at startup (visible in the logs).

2. Demo the extraction UI

  1. Navigate to http://localhost:8000/ and show the upload card with guidance badges.
  2. Drop in a digital NDA PDF (not scanned). Point out the loading spinner and toast feedback.
  3. Highlight colour-coded confidence and the evidence toggles for quick auditing.
